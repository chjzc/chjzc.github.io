ABOUT ME

Zhuochen Jin (金卓宸) is currently a Ph.D. student in Idvx Lab TJU(Tongji University), supervised by Prof. Nan Cao. He received bachelor's degree from ZJU (Zhejiang University) in major of mathematics. During his undergraduate studies, he was a member of the State Key Lab of CAD&CG.

His research interests lie in visual analytics and human-computer interaction. His work mainly focuses on visual analytics in the urban context.
